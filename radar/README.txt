MATERIALS REQUIRED:
-servo-motor
-HC-SR04
-esp8266
-wifi
-arduino IDE

CABLE CONNECTIONS:
-SERVO:
GND --> GND
VCC --> 3.3v
signal --> D4
- HC-SR04:
GND --> GND 
VCC --> 3.3v
trig --> D5
echo --> D6

Replace "YOUR_WIFI_SSID" to your wifi ssid (wifi name) and replace "YOUR_WIFI_PASSWORD" to your wifi password. After go to the serial monitor (if your serial monitor does not appear, click on tools and serial monitor) and copy ip adress in your navigator, make sure your esp is
connected to the same network as your device. Have fun with your radar ;).
